Sidebar #05

What is the concept of e-learning? What do you think of this concept? Answer:
E-learning can be ideally defined as: extending the concept of the teaching and learning process beyond the boundaries of the traditional classroom walls and launching to a rich multi-source environment in which interactive distance learning techniques will have a fundamental role in it so that the role of both the teacher and the learner is reformulated. ” That is, e-learning is not a substitute for the teacher but rather enhances its role as a supervisor, guide and organizer of the educational process, at a time when the learner takes the role of leadership and control in the educational process in terms of the time he wants to study, and choosing the appropriate study modes for him and his control of electronic operations from downloading or sending files Which leads to the development of his intellectual skills.
